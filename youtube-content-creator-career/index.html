About the Channel

The YouTube channel originally started out as a gaming and entertainment channel. After some time becoming "well established" some would argue, the channel took a turn into SaSS digital product sales. The channel turned into a marketing hub for video game enhancing software. This channel was able to yield 3% subscribers into monthly purchasers, this allowed not only the cost of the digital product to be covered but the developers & marketers to be covered.

Personal Reflection

At that time when I was 12 years old, I was not aware of the perspectives some people had on game enhancing software. Where I am today and daily seeking a deeper, more profound relationship with Christ. I am thankful to have grown out of that season of my life. Despite my career with my YouTube channel growing exponentially month over month both revenue and outreach, I deleted the channel to start fresh.

Biblical Fruit Through Experience

As I've continue to grow my relationship with Christ, It's been revealed to me the biblical fruit that has flourished from this business. I've been blessed to be experienced to strategical algorithm based analysis at a young age it has allowed me to navigate complex problems and help other individuals grow their ideas into realities. This business later fueled other businesses as I've grown older.
